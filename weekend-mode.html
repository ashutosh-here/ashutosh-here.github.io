Hurray it's Weekend :)

I like to spend my weekends relaxing and doing some things which give me positive vibes.
These include art, watching movies, learning new languages, heading out for short trips etc. I will try to finish the web series that I started watching way back ago.

I have two precious days that are dedicated only for me. Sometimes I start any new Project or continue unifinished Projects to enhance my technical skills. There are no deadlines, pressure or stress. I can decide what I want to build and start whenever I like. Even if I haven't finished it for one weekend, nothing bad can happen. I can always continue the next. For me personally, this part of the week has contributed the most to my personal development as a Software Developer.

Also it's weekend so I can make time for family and friends. I will go out with my family for dinner to spend quality time with them. One of my hobby is cycling , so occasionally I ride my bicycle in morning for some kilometers. I like to play badminton as well .
